Write a program that scans a given text file.
The program will notify any registered listeners with each word scanned
The types of listeners required are:
1 Word counter – that simply counts the total words sent to it
2 Number counter – that count the total numbers of string that represents numbers (for example "345", "0")
3 Longest word keeper – which keeps the last longest word sent to it
4 Reverse word – which reverse chars order in every given word
